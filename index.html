Crystal Collector!

How to play


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below, by clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it. Each time when the game starts the game will change the value of each crystal.
